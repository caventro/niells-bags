The crafting journey

Built loop by loop.

Crafted loop by loop, the design takes its cue from the very process of creation. It reflects the careful steps from yarn selection and bag shaping to intricate detailing and the perfect finish.

◉

Choose the yarn

Select a solid colour or create a bespoke mixed colour combination.

↻

Loop the shape

The shape comes to life as the body is meticulously hand-crocheted, building both its tactile texture and sturdy structure.

✦

Add the detail

Handles, tassels, pearls and finishing touches are carefully added to give every bag its own character.

✓

Finish the piece

Once complete, the bag is inspected, shaped, and made ready for its new owner.
